About the Warriors

Golden State Warriors adalah tim bola basket profesional yang berbasis di San Francisco, California. Mereka adalah anggota Divisi Pasifik Wilayah Barat di NBA. Dikenal karena serangan cepatnya, Warriors memiliki sejarah yang kaya, memenangkan banyak kejuaraan.

Key Players

  • Stephen Curry - Point Guard
  • Klay Thompson - Shooting Guard
  • Draymond Green - Power Forward
  • Andrew Wiggins - Small Forward

Championship Titles

The Golden State Warriors have won a total of 7 NBA championships, with titles in 1947, 1956, 1975, 2015, 2017, 2018, and 2022.

Chase Center

Chase Center is an indoor arena in the Mission Bay neighborhood of San Francisco, California. It is the home of the Golden State Warriors of the National Basketball Association.
